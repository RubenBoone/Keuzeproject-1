Actie MIN, there to help!

Actie MIN helps people in poverty in the Mol, Balen, Dessel and Reties regions.

Contact us by phone 0473/241361.
Contact us by mail: actiemenseninnood@gmail.com

While supplies last/while stock last.

People who don’t have enough money to buy food can come over for a first conversation. If we can help you, you’ll get a membership card which you can use in our store OP=OP. This store is the place where we distribute food to our members.

Our store ‘OP=OP’ can be found at the back of the restaurant Apollon, located at st. Apollonioalaan 198 in Mol.

Food distribution, with fresh fruits and vegetables:

  • Members only
  • Takes place in our store at the address st. Apollonioalaan 198, Mol
  • Each saturday between 8:30 am and noon
  • Last registration at 11:30 am
  • First register in the waiting room
  • Cost: 2€ per family
